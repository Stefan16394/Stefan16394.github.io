Twitch TV

Twitch is a live streaming video platform owned by Twitch Interactive, now a subsidiary of Amazon.com.Introduced in June 2011 as a spin-off of the general-interest streaming platform, Justin.tv, the site primarily focuses on video gaming, including playthroughs of video games, broadcasts of eSports competitions, creative content, and more recently, music broadcasts. Content on the site can either be viewed live or via video on demand. The popularity of Twitch would eclipse that of its general-interest counterpart; in October 2013, the website had 45 million unique viewers,and by February 2014, it was considered the fourth largest source of peak Internet traffic in the United States.
